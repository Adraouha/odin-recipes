Perfect Pancakes

Description

Serve these pancakes with butter and syrup, or up the ante with toppings such as sprinkles, chopped bittersweet chocolate, and sliced or chopped fruit for a perfect breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • /4 cup white vinegar
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon butter, softened, or as needed, for greasing, plus more for serving
  • toppings of choice, e.g. maple syrup or fruit, for serving

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. SPreheat a griddle to 300 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Step 2

    3. Stir together milk and vinegar in a small bowl or a 4-cup liquid measuring cup.

  3. Step 3

    4. Whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a second bowl.

  4. Step 4

    5. Beat eggs in a large bowl until smooth. Add vanilla and milk mixture; whisk together. Add flour mixture to bowl with egg mixture and whisk until just combined. (Batter will be lumpy.) Let rest 3 minutes and gently stir again.

  5. Step 5

    6. Melt some butter on the griddle, then carefully wipe with a paper towel. (You want just a bit of a sheen; donâ€™t overgrease the griddle.) Spoon batter in 1/3-cup portions onto griddle. Cook until bubbles form on surface of batter, about 2 1/2 minutes.

  6. Step 6

    7. MFlip pancakes and cook until browned on the other side, about 1 1/2 minutes more. Repeat with remaining batter. Serve immediately with butter and toppings.
